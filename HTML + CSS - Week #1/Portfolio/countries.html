Francia

France is a Western European country known for its culture, art, and cuisine. Its capital is Paris, famous for landmarks like the Eiffel Tower. France is also renowned for its history, fashion, and diverse landscapes.

Italy

Italy is located in southern Europe and is famous for its rich historical and cultural heritage. Its capital is Rome, home to important monuments such as the Colosseum. Italy is known for its food, art, and architecture.

Russia

Russia is the world's largest country, located between Europe and Asia. Its capital is Moscow. Russia is notable for its vast territory, its history, and its cultural and political influence.
